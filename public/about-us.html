Meet the Team

Executive Coordinator

David Dias


David is a Communication Networks Engineer, finishing a Masters on Cloud Computing, P2P networks and Mobile applications at IST. He serves Computer Society as R8 MDC Executive Coordinator. David lives his days trying to push forward the humanity by being a catalyst of his peers, trying to make the most of the energy he puts in every project he is up too, whether it is related to his field of studies or not. You can find David motivating everyone around me to make something happen, or trying to develop the next big thing in WebDev.

Ioannis is a senior student at the University of Thessaly, Department of Computer Science and Biomedical Informatics and his main interests are Biomedical Engineering and Web Development.
He has been an active IEEE member since 2011 and has been member of the Organizing Committe of many events since then. Currently, he is Chairman of the IEEE UTH (Lamia) Student Branch, Ad Hoc Member of the R8 Pre-University Works Sub-Committee and Operations Coordinator of the R8 Computer Society Member Development Committee.
